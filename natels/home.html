Rare Natural Element Data

Rare natural element occurrences (EOs) are plant and animal populations, natural plant communities, bird nesting colonies, caves and other elements in nature that are important for biodiversity conservation.

Making a list of rare natural elements for
Cumberland Island using QGIS and LibreOffice Calc

A demonstration of one way to make an EO (element occurrence) list of rare species and natural plant communities for a location. In this case we use free software, QGIS and LibreOffice Calc. The list generated is for Cumberland Island, Georgia.
